About Us

Scholarshome Shahi Eidgah started its activities in 2002 and shifted to its own building at Shahi Eidgah 2006. Our Campus is able to accommodate over 1,500 students! Under the current administration of our Retired Colonel Principal Munir Ahmed Quadery, we have organized many new departments to ensure the campus life for our students become vibrant and memorable.
